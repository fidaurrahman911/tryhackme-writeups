# Key Takeaways

Logs are one of the most valuable sources of evidence during cybersecurity investigations. Every action performed on a system can leave traces in logs, making them essential for detecting attacks, investigating incidents, monitoring infrastructure, and maintaining organizational security.

---

# Why This Matters for Cybersecurity

Security Operations Centers (SOCs) rely heavily on logs to detect attacks in real time. SIEM platforms such as Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Elastic, and QRadar continuously analyze logs to identify suspicious behavior, generate alerts, and support threat hunting. A strong understanding of log analysis is therefore a fundamental skill for SOC Analysts, Incident Responders, and Digital Forensics professionals.
